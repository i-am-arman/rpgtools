Welcome to the Encounter Generator! Based on (but expanding on) the tables in the Adventurer, Conquerer, King System's Core book and Lair's and Encounters, this randomly generates a full encounter. Not all encounters make sense based on the territory; feel free to keep re-rolling until something that fits the story shows up. If any part of this tool doesn't make sense, or you have more questions about what you can and can't do, please purchase the Adventurer, Conquerer, King books - they really are an awesome resource!
